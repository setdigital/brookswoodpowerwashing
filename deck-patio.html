Do I need to clean my deck before staining?

Yes. Your deck is frequently exposed to natural elements and airborne debris and dust. Without a proper deck wash and bleaching, you will be only sealing in dirt and debris.
A reputed cleaning service like Brookswood will get rid of mildew, algae, and greying of the deck. This prepares your deck for staining.

What has caused weeds and moss in my patio and driveways?

When the sand between blocks drop, dirt and mud get in. particular climates and such mud between the blocks is good for the growth of the vegetation.

You can slow down this process by keeping the sand between the joints and sweeping the patio regularly. Also, once you clean your patio with Brookswood, you can seal it and eliminate this weed problem.

Does pressure washing damage the pointing between patio slabs?

A pressure wash cannot affect the well-maintained pointing. If it is already weak or damaged, high-psi washing can blow it out.
